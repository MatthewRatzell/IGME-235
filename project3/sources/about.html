Duck Hunt Simulator

High Concept

Based off the groundbreaking NES game Duck Hunt, the user looks to shoot and kill ducks; however, this time around the user will have to lead their shot as if it were a real hunting simulator, using the money received the user will also be able to purchase different shotshell spreads, and shotguns to increase their chances of bagging as many ducks as possible.

Genre

The genre is shooter/hunting simulator

Platform

Planning on desktop only but we will see how it responds on mobile

Story

You are a competitive duck hunter attempting to bag as many ducks as you can in an outing, using the shot spread and shotgun of your choice you must bag more ducks(dont worry these ducks are an invasive species) than the other competitors to earn the most money to further advance your set-up

Aestetics

Graphic style I would say pixel art, drawing inspiration from the classic NES Duck Hunt

Gameplay

Players will control where they shoot and prior to shooting they will have the option to purchase different shotgun shells and shotguns to change how the shooting works
